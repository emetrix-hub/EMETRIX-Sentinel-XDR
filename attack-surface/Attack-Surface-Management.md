# EMETRIX Sentinel XDR Attack Surface Management Platform (ASM)

## Executive Summary

The Attack Surface Management (ASM) Platform continuously discovers, inventories, classifies, monitors, and secures all externally exposed assets across the internet, cloud, hybrid infrastructure, subsidiaries, third parties, SaaS platforms, APIs, mobile applications, and domains.

The platform combines External Attack Surface Management (EASM), Cyber Asset Attack Surface Management (CAASM), Exposure Management, Digital Risk Protection, and AI-driven attack path analysis into a unified security capability.
