# EMETRIX Sentinel XDR AI SOC Analyst

## Executive Summary

The AI SOC Analyst is the autonomous intelligence layer of EMETRIX Sentinel XDR. It continuously monitors security telemetry, triages alerts, correlates events, investigates incidents, recommends remediation, executes approved playbooks, and assists human analysts through natural language interaction.

The platform functions as an AI-powered Tier 1, Tier 2, and assistant for Tier 3 SOC operations while maintaining human oversight for high-impact decisions.
