# EMETRIX Sentinel XDR Executive Security Analytics

## Executive Summary

The Executive Security Analytics Platform transforms technical cybersecurity telemetry into executive-level business intelligence. It delivers real-time security KPIs, cyber risk scoring, board reporting, financial impact analysis, security maturity measurements, predictive forecasting, and AI-generated executive recommendations.

The platform is designed for CISOs, CIOs, executives, board members, compliance officers, and business leadership.
