# EMETRIX Sentinel XDR Compliance & Governance Platform

## Executive Summary

The Compliance & Governance Platform provides continuous governance, regulatory compliance monitoring, automated evidence collection, policy management, audit readiness, risk reporting, and executive oversight across hybrid, cloud, and on-premise environments.

The platform continuously evaluates security controls against international standards while generating automated evidence for audits and certifications.
