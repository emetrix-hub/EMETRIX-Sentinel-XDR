# EMETRIX Sentinel XDR Digital Forensics Platform

## Executive Summary

The Digital Forensics Platform provides enterprise-grade forensic investigation capabilities for Windows, Linux, macOS, cloud workloads, containers, mobile devices, and memory analysis. It enables investigators to collect, preserve, analyze, and report digital evidence while maintaining full chain of custody.
