# EMETRIX Sentinel XDR Identity Threat Detection & Response Platform

## Executive Summary

The Identity Threat Detection & Response (ITDR) Platform protects enterprise identities against credential theft, account compromise, privilege abuse, insider threats, and identity-based attacks. It combines identity telemetry, AI-powered behavioral analytics, Zero Trust principles, and automated response capabilities.

# Objectives

- Continuous identity monitoring
- Identity risk scoring
- Credential theft detection
- Privileged account monitoring
- Identity anomaly detection
