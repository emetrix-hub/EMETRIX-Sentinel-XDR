# EPIC-001 — Product Vision & Strategy

---

# Executive Summary

EMETRIX Sentinel XDR is an enterprise-grade AI-powered Extended Detection and Response (XDR) platform designed to unify cybersecurity operations into a single intelligent ecosystem.

Unlike traditional SIEM solutions that only collect logs, EMETRIX Sentinel XDR combines:

- SIEM
- SOAR
- Threat Intelligence
- AI Security Copilot
- Detection Engineering
- Incident Response
- Cloud Security
- Identity Protection
- Compliance Management
- Physical Security Integration

The platform enables organizations to detect, investigate, respond to, and automate cyber threats through a modern AI-first architecture.

---

# Vision Statement

Become the next-generation AI Security Operations Platform trusted by enterprises worldwide.

---

# Mission Statement

Deliver intelligent cybersecurity operations through automation, AI assistance, and unified visibility while reducing detection and response time across every security domain.

---

# Product Goals

- Reduce Mean Time To Detect (MTTD)
- Reduce Mean Time To Respond (MTTR)
- Provide a unified SOC platform
- Replace multiple disconnected security products
- Deliver enterprise-grade scalability
- Simplify analyst workflows
- Integrate physical and cyber security
