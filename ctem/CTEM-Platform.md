# EMETRIX Sentinel XDR Continuous Threat Exposure Management (CTEM)

## Executive Summary

The Continuous Threat Exposure Management (CTEM) Platform continuously discovers, validates, prioritizes, and remediates cyber exposure across the enterprise. It combines attack surface management, vulnerability intelligence, identity exposure, cloud posture, threat intelligence, and business context into a single exposure management framework.

The platform follows Gartner's CTEM methodology while integrating AI-driven prioritization and autonomous remediation workflows.
