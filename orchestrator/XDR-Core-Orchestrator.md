# EMETRIX Sentinel XDR Core Orchestrator

## Executive Summary

The XDR Core Orchestrator is the central nervous system of EMETRIX Sentinel XDR. It coordinates communication, workflows, telemetry, AI reasoning, automation, and data exchange across every security module within the platform.

It enables unified operations across SIEM, EDR, ITDR, CNAPP, SOAR, Threat Intelligence, Digital Forensics, Threat Hunting, Compliance, Executive Analytics, AI SOC Analyst, and future AI agents.
