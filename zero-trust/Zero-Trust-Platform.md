# EMETRIX Sentinel XDR Zero Trust Platform

## Executive Summary

The Zero Trust Platform enforces continuous verification of every user, device, workload, application, API, and network connection regardless of location.

Built around the principles of "Never Trust, Always Verify", the platform combines identity intelligence, adaptive authentication, continuous risk assessment, least privilege access, microsegmentation, device trust, behavioral analytics, and AI-driven policy decisions.
