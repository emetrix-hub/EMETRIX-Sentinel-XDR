# EMETRIX Sentinel XDR Data Security Posture Management Platform (DSPM)

## Executive Summary

The Data Security Posture Management (DSPM) Platform continuously discovers, classifies, maps, monitors, and protects sensitive data across cloud, on-premises, SaaS, hybrid, containers, databases, data lakes, and AI environments.

The platform provides enterprise-wide visibility into where sensitive data resides, who has access, how it is used, and what risks threaten its confidentiality, integrity, and availability.
